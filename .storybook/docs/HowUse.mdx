## How to use Underglaze?

One way is using headless React components with default styles, which can be easily embedded in your decentralized application (dapp). These components are designed to be highly customizable, allowing you to tailor them to your specific needs.

Our advanced software development kit (SDK) and flexible modules make it easy to build fully composable and social experiences. You can choose from a wide variety of features and functionalities to create the perfect user experience for your customers. Plus, our team of experienced developers is always here to help if you need any assistance along the way.

To generate the necessary TypeScript code, you can also utilize the functionality available within our dapp dashboard. This feature streamlines the process and allows for a more efficient integration of our Underglaze Protocol into your decentralized application.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, our tools and resources are designed to make your job easier. With our innovative solutions, you can create cutting-edge dapps that stand out from the competition and deliver real value to your users.
