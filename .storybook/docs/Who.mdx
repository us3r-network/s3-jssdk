## Who is Underglaze for?

The following individuals and organizations may be interested in integrating decentralized user login and user data management within their projects:

- Project owners, who want to enhance their platform's security and resilience to censorship measures while also providing a seamless user experience.
- Developers, who want to add a layer of decentralization to their projects, allowing for greater transparency and control over user data.
- Enterprises, recognize the importance of maintaining public data and security while still being able to access and utilize valuable user data.
- Startups, who want to jumpstart their business with a strong foundation of trust and security by implementing decentralized user login and data management from the beginning.
